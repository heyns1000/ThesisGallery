Wouter (Your Current Hub): is fantastic. It's minimalist, extremely fast, and perfect for many applications. Its simplicity is its greatest strength.

React Router (The VaultKey Example): is the industry standard for large, complex applications. Its strength lies in its ability to handle nested layouts (like a main header/footer with changing content inside) and complex routing scenarios, which are inevitable in an app with thousands of pages.

For a project of your scale—the Fruitful Global Master Hub—sticking with a simple router will eventually become a bottleneck. You will spend more time fighting the tool to create complex layouts than you would building features.
